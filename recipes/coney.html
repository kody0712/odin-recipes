Classic Coney Dog Sauce

Desciprition

The classic coney sauce is easy to make and taste so delicious anyone in the family will love it!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 1/2 cups of ketchup
  • 1/4 cup of white sugar
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/4 cup prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/3 teaspoon celery seed
  • 3/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Place ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook, stirring to crumble, until beef is browned.
  3. Drain grease from skillet.
  4. Transfer beef and onions to a slow cooker and stire in ketchup, sugar, vinegar and mustard.
  5. Season with celery seed, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt.
  6. Cover and simmer on Low for a few hours before serving.
