Parmesan Chicken

Description

The chicken parmesan is done casserole style. It consists of delicious tender chicken, a crunchy cheese coating, and flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed and finley chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 cups prepared marinara sauce
  • 1.4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 (5 ounce) package garlic croutons

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Coat bottom of 9x13 casserole dish with olive oil, and sprinkle garlic and red pepper flakes.
  3. Arrange chicken in bottom of dish.
  4. Pour marinara sauce over chicken.
  5. Sprinkle basil over marinara sauce, and top off with mozzarella cheese.
  6. Followed by half parmesan cheese.
  7. Sprinkle on croutons then top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until cheese and croutons are golden brown and chicken is no longer pink.
  9. Between 30 minutes to an hour. Check chicken with thermometer. Thickest part of chicken should be at least 160 degrees F.
