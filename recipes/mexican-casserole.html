Mexican Casserole

Desciprition

An easy and tasty dish that can be made quickly for the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cups salsa
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, drained
  • 3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 2 cups shredded cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Brown ground beef over medium-high heat.
  3. Stir in salsa, reduce heat, and simmer 20 minutes, or until liquid is absorbed.
  4. Stir in beans and heat through.
  5. Spray a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray.
  6. Spread crushed tortilla chips in dish.
  7. Spoon beef mixture over chips.
  8. Spread sour cream over beef
  9. Sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomato over sour cream.
  10. Top with cheddar cheese.
